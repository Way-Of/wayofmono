### 4.2 Inspection Rights
Investor may inspect books, records, and facilities upon reasonable notice, and meet with management.

### 4.3 Protective Provisions
Investor consent required (majority of Preferred Stock) for:
- **Corporate Actions**: Liquidation, asset sale, merger, change of control
- **Equity**: New series with superior rights, equity issuances causing dilution, Charter/Bylaws amendments, stock repurchases
- **Financial**: Budget approval, debt exceeding [X]x ARR, dividends, related-party transactions > $[X,XXX]
- **Organizational**: Board size changes, change in line of business

### 4.4 Rights of First Refusal (ROFR)
Investor has ROFR to purchase pro rata share of any new equity issuance (with standard exceptions for employee pool, convertible securities, strategic partnerships).

### 4.5 Co-Sale (Tag-Along) Rights
If Founders or major shareholders (>[X]%) sell shares, Investor may participate pro rata.

### 4.6 Drag-Along Rights
Company may require Investor to participate in a sale approved by majority of Preferred Stock.

## 7. Anti-Dilution Protections

**Broad-Based Weighted Average** anti-dilution protection:
- Adjusted conversion price = (CP × OS + NM) / (OS + NS)
- CP = Current conversion price
- OS = Fully diluted shares outstanding
- NM = New money raised
- NS = New shares issued
- Exceptions: Employee pool (up to [X]%/yr), acquisitions, convertible securities

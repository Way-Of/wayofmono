## Presentation Tips

- **Know your audience**: Tailor technical depth to investor background
- **Have backup slides ready**: For deep-dive questions on technology, market data, financial model
- **Time yourself**: Practice to stay within allotted time
- **Let data speak**: Use your metrics as proof points, not aspirations
- **The ask is a conversation starter**: Be prepared to negotiate terms

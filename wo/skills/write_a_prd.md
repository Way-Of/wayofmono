# Write a PRD

This skill helps transform a rough idea or client brief into a formal Product Requirements Document.

## Sections
- **Problem Statement:** What pain are we solving?
- **User Personas:** Who is this for?
- **Functional Requirements:** What must it do?
- **Non-Functional Requirements:** Security, performance, scale.
- **Success Metrics:** How do we measure victory?
